Prints for Collectors

The Klemantaski Collection owns the original negatives for all of its over 500,000 images, together with their international copyright. We are able to make prints of any of our images in whatever size or style a customer may request.

The Venue List and The Race Program List

Given the size of the collection, we do not have a specific and detailed catalog showing or describing all of our images. Nevertheless, each image does have a unique Reference Number which allows us to locate the negative in our archive and by which customers may order prints. To assist us and our customers, we maintain what we call a Venue List which sets forth, by year, all the motorsports and other automotive events from which we have photographs, including the approximate quantity of b&w and/or color images from each event. We also maintain an extensive collection of race programs and related documents in our library. Our Program List is also available for download. Copies of our race programs are generally available by email without charge.

Ordering Prints

For general information on ordering prints, please see How to Order Prints which may be obtained on our Downloads page. If you already know the Reference Number of the image you would like to order, please use our Blank Order Form which is on The Gallery page.

We have a limited inventory of prints which were signed by some of our photographers. In a search in The Gallery, if we have a signed print in stock there will be a line of text so indicating below the larger version of the image. If you are interested in a signed print, please contact us for more information.

If we are given a particular race, rally or other event and car identity (the car's racing number or license plate number, if any, will be helpful but is not absolutely necessary), we will be able to tell you what photographs are available in our archive. Similarly, if you wish to see photographs of a certain driver, our extensive reference library of race programs and books can usually direct us to the appropriate races where that driver participated.

Identifying the Images You Want

In order to assist our customers in the identification of images which they may wish to purchase as prints and which are not otherwise reproduced in the The Gallery or in one of our Special Publications, we are able to supply you with low resolution image scans via email in modest quantities without charge from which you may make a selection to receive a print. If you have a particular area of interest (a race, a specific car, a certain driver, etc.) for which you would like to see some of our images, you may contact us by Email or by letter, telephone or fax .
